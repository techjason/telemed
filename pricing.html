  • Is there a free plan?

    No. At the moment, we are not offering a free plan. You can go to tabmed.hk/demo and try our demo bot or contact us for a live demo from our team.

  • What is 1 message?

    1 message credit is equal to 1 complete A.I. generated response.

  • Can I get more chatbots?

    You can get additional chatbots with our ultimate plan (10 chatbots + additional chatbots charged per unit per month) and custom plan.

  • Can I get more message credits?

    Yes - you can add message credits manually or automatically when you run out. Each message credit costs about ~ $0.01 USD / HKD $0.08.

  • How secure is the data that I upload?

    All data uploaded to any Tabmed account is stored in a 256-bit encrypted database that is only accessible to the users you decide. Your data will never be used or sold to 3rd parties.

  • How does the chatbot know everything about me / my business / my organisation?

    When creating your chatbot, Tabmed AI will ask you to enter the URL of the website you want the A.I. to learn from. Within seconds, the chatbot will know everything about you / your business / your organisation and be able to relay the information in a conversation format.

    Additionally, you can upload a .pdf, .txt, .doc, or .docx files. containing information that you would like your chatbot to know. You can also copy/paste a raw text file and a Q&A.

  • What if my website is not up to date / I don't have a website?

    This isn't a problem! If your website does not have up-to-date information about your products, services, or pricing, you can update the Tabmed AI knowledge-base with accurate information through text or document files (.pdf, .txt, .doc, .docs files). Then when asked, your chatbot will respond with the information you provided and override what is on-your website. This way, we can guarantee that your chatbot always responds with 100% accuracy.

  • What types of content are acceptable for training the chatbot?

    Tabmed AI is versatile and accommodates a variety of content types to train the chatbot. This includes website content, direct text input, and various file formats such as .pdf, .txt, .doc, or .docx. The richness and breadth of your provided content can significantly enhance the chatbot's ability to respond accurately and comprehensively to queries. The more content you provide, the more equipped the chatbot becomes!

  • How can I integrate the chatbot into my website?

    Tabmed AI provides an intuitive and efficient process to incorporate the chatbot into your website. Each chatbot is associated with a distinct URL. You can use this URL to seamlessly embed the chatbot on your website via the iframe code that we furnish. If preferred, you can also establish a direct hyperlink to the chatbot from your site. The process is designed for optimal user convenience.

    For 1-on-1 live guidance on embedding the chat bot onto your website or app, feel free to contact us.

  • How do I embed the chatbot into my app?

    Integrating Tabmed AI's chatbot into your application is a straightforward process. For iOS applications, you can utilize the WebView component from the WebKit framework to embed the chatbot through an iframe. This allows your application to display web content directly within your app.

    For Android applications, you can use the WebView widget available in Android's API. Similar to iOS, you can use this widget to display web content, including iframes, directly within your app.

    For 1-on-1 live guidance on embedding the chat bot onto your website or app, feel free to contact us.

  • Does Tabmed AI support websites in languages other than English and Chinese?

    Absolutely. Tabmed AI is designed with a global user base in mind and has the capability to work with websites in a variety of languages, not just limited to English and Chinese. The chatbot's output language can be adjusted by refining the prompt to cater to your specific language requirements.

    Furthermore, our chatbot has the ability to answer queries posed in a multitude of languages. This means you can ask questions in your preferred language, and our chatbot will respond accordingly.

  • Is there scope for customizing my chatbot?

    Absolutely. You can personalize elements such as the color scheme, the chatbot icon, and the name of the chatbot to align with your brand identity. Additionally, you have the flexibility to adjust font sizes to ensure an optimal user experience.
